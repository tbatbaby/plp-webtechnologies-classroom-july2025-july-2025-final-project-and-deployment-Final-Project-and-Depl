About Elegance Boutique

Our story, mission, and the team behind your favorite fashion destination

Our Story

Founded in 2019, TARA O CLOTHINGS began as a small boutique in the heart of New York City. Our founder, RAHEEM MOTUNRAYO TAIBAT, had a vision to create a space where fashion, jewelry, and premium fabrics could coexist in perfect harmony.

What started as a single store has now grown into a beloved brand known for its commitment to quality, attention to detail, and exceptional customer service. We carefully curate each piece in our collection, working with talented designers and artisans from around the world.

Today, we continue to honor our founding principles while embracing innovation and sustainability in everything we do.
